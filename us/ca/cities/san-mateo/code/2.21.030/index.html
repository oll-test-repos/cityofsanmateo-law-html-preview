2.21.030 POWERS—DUTIES.

The Community Relations Commission shall have the following powers and duties:

(a) To conduct public hearings pursuant to Chapter 7.16.

(b) To hear appeals for administrative citations pursuant to Section 1.10.060, except for administrative citation appeals relating to violations of Chapter 7.14, 23.06 through 23.56, Chapter 23.70, and Chapter 23.72 of this Code.

(c) To study, investigate, review policies, and hold public hearings to advance the City's equity and inclusion objectives by making recommendations to the City Council.

(d) To provide oversight of Federal, State or other grant programs that implement public service, housing, and community development programs, and make recommendations on those programs which may be assigned to them by the Council.

(e) To work with organizations of similar intent formulated by public agencies having jurisdiction within the City and with the Human Relations Commission of the County. To this end, a member shall be assigned as liaison to each group and shall attend the meetings thereof and report thereon to the Commission. Any person assigned as liaison from the County Commission, or other such group, to this Community Relations Commission shall be an ex officio member but shall have no vote.

(f) Such other powers and duties as may be prescribed from time to time by ordinance or resolution of the Council.

(g) The Community Relations Commission shall make recommendations to the Council to address, reduce or eliminate existing inequalities and disadvantages and prepare, encourage and coordinate programs of inclusion to insure the complete utilization of the human resources of this community for the betterment of all its inhabitants.

(Ord. No. 2024-7 § 1; Ord. No. 2022-12 § 4; Ord. No. 2013-2 § 2; Ord. No. 2006-2 § 1; Ord. No. 1977-23 § 1; Ord. No. 1973-18 § 2; Ord. No. 1972-20 § 2; prior code § 10.5.03.)
